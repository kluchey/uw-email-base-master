You can make this reunion especially memorable for you and your classmates as a volunteer Class Rep. In this important role, you will help organize events specifically for your individual class, department and year. Contact Gosia Brestovacki Senior Alumni Officer 519-888-4567, ext. 36838
Learn more about how Waterloo Engineering is going to Educate the Engineer of the Future. Check out the walk-through of the much anticipated Engineering 7 which has just broke ground on November 12th.
Mark your calendar now and plan to come back to campus for Reunion weekend on Saturday, October 1 and Sunday, October 2, 2016.
You’re invited to the exclusive Waterloo Engineering reunion to reconnect with classmates, discover why we’re (still) Canada’s premier engineering school and see for yourself how we are, once again, disrupting engineering education. You will witness firsthand the phenomenal growth of Waterloo Engineering, hear the widely anticipated Dean’s address and attend the special back-to-the-classroom lectures. It’s a weekend of revisiting your younger days and remembering the years of personal dedication it took to become a Waterloo engineer.
